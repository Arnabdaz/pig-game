How to play:

  1. Start rolling the dice by clicking the 🎲 ROLL DICE button. your scores will be added on the current score box
  2. Now if you encounter a 1 rolled by the dice then your current score becomes 0 and the dice will be passed to the next player
  3. If you press the 📥 HOLD button when there is some score (not 0) then that score will be added to your total score and the dice will be passed to the other player
  4. The player which gets 100 or more in total score first wins the game
  5. To restart the game Just press 🔄️ NEW GAME button
